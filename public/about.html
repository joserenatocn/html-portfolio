About Me

Hi, I’m José Renato. I grew up in Brasília, Brazil, and these days I’m living in New York City. When I’m not working or studying, I’m usually making music (piano, guitar, or singing), out for a run, or training for a triathlon. I’ve always been curious about technology, but for a long time I thought coding was something I’d “missed the chance” to learn. Turns out it’s never too late — so here I am.

I finished medical school in 2022 and moved to New York for residency. Along the way, I got really interested in artificial intelligence and how it could change the future of healthcare. I started learning Python and exploring data science and machine learning. To my surprise, I found I actually enjoy the process of coding itself, which got me branching out into web development too.

This website is one of my first little projects. It’s simple on purpose — a place to practice the basics. As I keep learning, I’ll use it to share my progress and whatever new things I manage to build.
